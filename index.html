Dragons

The Mythical Creature

Dragons, the legendary beasts of myth, symbolise power, wisdom, and destruction. Across cultures, they soar through ancient tales as majestic guardians, fierce adversaries, or even benevolent deities shaping the world.

From Eastern serpentine beings embodying harmony to Western fire-breathing terrors, dragons ignite imaginations with their formidable presence. They hoard treasure in dark caverns, command storms with a roar, and test heroes seeking glory. As timeless figures, dragons remain a core motif in fantasy, their scales gleaming in legends, games, and dreams.

Knight

The Medieval Warrior

The knight embodies honour, duty, and unwavering resolve.

Clad in steel, they ride into battle with steadfast loyalty, wielding sword and shield to defend kingdoms. Bound by codes of chivalry, knights uphold justice, their legends echoing through history.

Werewolf

The Night Terror

The werewolf embodies primal rage and transformation.

Cursed under the full moon, a werewolf is both predator and victim, torn between humanity and beast. Their monstrous strength, razor-sharp claws, and eerie howl strike terror into the night, while their struggle for control adds tragedy to their legend. Feared and hunted, they stalk the dark, trapped in an endless cycle of fury and remorse.

Deadpool

The Anti-Hero

Deadpool is chaos wrapped in red spandex, wielding swords, sarcasm, and a knack for breaking the fourth wall with reckless abandon.

A rogue experiment turned mercenary, Wade Wilson battles foes with unmatched agility and endless wisecracks. His healing factor makes him nearly unstoppable, while his unpredictable nature keeps allies and enemies alike on edge. Whether saving the day or causing mayhem, Deadpool is both hero and menace, redefining antiheroes with style, humour, and absurdity.
